title=ADHD Trait
description=[h1]ADHD Trait[/h1]
description=Your brain runs in permanent overdrive. Everything is faster — but stillness kills.
description=
description=[h2]Features[/h2]
description=[list]
description=[*][b]Everything is faster.[/b] All timed actions — crafting, reading, foraging, ripping clothes, disassembling — complete [b]3x faster[/b] by default (configurable 1–10x). You also move faster, with boosted Fitness, Sprinting and Nimble.
description=[*][b]Stillness kills.[/b] Stand still too long and your character drops dead — and gets back up as a zombie. The idle limit defaults to [b]15 seconds[/b] and is fully configurable.
description=[*][b]You'll hear it coming.[/b] For the final seconds (default 5, configurable) a [b]loud alarm blares[/b] and a panicked countdown flashes over your head: "no no no NO— I have to MOVE!"
description=[*][b]Proper trait.[/b] Custom lightning-bolt icon, shown in character creation (Negative Traits, +6 points) and on the character info panel.
description=[*][b]Forceable.[/b] Server admins can force the trait onto specific players — or everyone — from Sandbox Options. It auto-selects in the creation screen and snaps back if deselected.
description=[/list]
description=
description=[h2]What counts as moving?[/h2]
description=The idle timer resets while you walk, run, drive, craft, read, aim or attack. Sitting in a [b]parked[/b] car counts as standing still — keep it moving. The countdown uses real wall-clock time: pausing and fast-forwarding do not advance it.
description=
description=When the timer runs out you are set fully Knox-infected and killed, so the corpse reanimates through the game's normal infection pipeline.
description=
description=[h2]Sandbox Options (ADHD page)[/h2]
description=[list]
description=[*][b]Action speed multiplier[/b] — 1–10, default 3
description=[*][b]Seconds standing still before death[/b] — 1–600, default 15
description=[*][b]Seconds of warning before death[/b] — 1–60, default 5
description=[*][b]Forced usernames[/b] — comma-separated list, * = everyone, empty = optional. In singleplayer any non-empty value forces the trait.
description=[/list]
description=
description=Works on both [b]Build 41[/b] and [b]Build 42[/b].
